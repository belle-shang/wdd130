Trip Plans with introductions

Half-Day Excursion

This is a perfect trip plan for beginners or those with limited time. It includes a 3-4 hour rafting adventure on a scenic and gentle stretch of river. This trip plan will give you a taste of white water rafting, without being too overwhelming.

Full-Day Adventure

For those who want to spend more time on the water, a full-day rafting adventure may be the way to go. This trip plan typically includes 6-7 hours of rafting, with a break for lunch in the middle of the day. This plan is ideal for those who want to experience a longer, more challenging ride.

Multi-Day Expedition

For the truly adventurous, a multi-day rafting expedition is the ultimate trip plan. This type of plan includes camping overnight along the river and rafting several sections of the river over a few days. This plan is perfect for those who want to fully immerse themselves in the rafting experience.

Customized Trip

If none of the above plans suit your needs, you can always create a customized trip plan that fits your preferences. You can work with the rafting company to tailor the trip to your group's size, skill level, and desired itinerary. This plan is ideal for groups or families with specific requirements or desires.
